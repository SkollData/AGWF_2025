# SITE.md — Análisis Geoestadístico rumbo al 2027

## 1. Vision

A premium, data-driven web platform for electoral geostatistical analysis targeting the 2027 Mexican elections. The site serves as a comprehensive dashboard and information hub for strategic decision-making, territorial intelligence, and field operations management.
